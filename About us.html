About us

Our Goals

At hotbeans, our number #1 goal is creating skilled developers.
We encourage people to learn to be a web developer and give aspiring
developers experience while working as a trainee at our company.

Our History

Hotbeans was founded by an American college student, called Tobey Maguire,
in 2019. At first growth was slow with us constantly struggling to find work
and make a name for ourselves. However, in 2020 tragedy struck the world
when the coronavirus forced many countries into lockdown. Our rate of
employment had increased by 300% as many businesses which had
previously sold their products only in their stores had to go online to
maintain social distancing laws during lockdown.

Since then, we had more work than we were equipped to handle so our focus
had shifted to a mass employment. This has also been working in our favour
as many people who lost their jobs these last few years jumped at the
opportunity.

In summary, Hotbeans is doing better than ever before, and we will strive to
be even greater in the future.
